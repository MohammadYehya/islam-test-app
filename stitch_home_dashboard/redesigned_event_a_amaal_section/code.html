history_edu About the Day

Eid al-Mab'ath marks the significant day when Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) was officially appointed as the Messenger of Allah. At the age of 40, while meditating in the Cave of Hira, the first revelation of the Holy Quran was bestowed upon him through Angel Jibra'eel.

This event transformed human history, signaling the end of the age of ignorance (Jahiliyyah) and the beginning of the final divine guidance for humanity. It is a day of gratitude, reflection, and celebration of the Prophet's mercy.
